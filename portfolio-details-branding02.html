Project information

  • Category: Design for Sustainability
  • Client: Friends of the Earth
  • Project date: 20 May, 2022

For this project, you will develop a detailed, logical, hierarchical research reference on aspects of Design for Sustainability. The purpose of this project is to develop content and concepts for the projects that will follow. The content map should be a useful, organized synthesis of the research you conduct about the topic, and can refer to sustainability issues in any design discipline. To develop a detailed, logical, hierarchical research reference on your selected topic centered on Friends of the Earth perspective.

BROCHURE

The goal of this project is to provide a detailed analysis of an aspect of our topic for your client Friends of the Earth in an engaging, memorable, printed format by describing and “diagramming” a particular quantifiable aspect of your topic/subject. The purpose of this project is also to further expand or translate your design system to take advantage of the tactile, material nature of print.

ANIMATION

My goal for this project is to design an animated graphic that highlights a significant aspect of your chosen site to further generate public interest, and advocate for the site’s conservation. The purpose of this project is to expand your design palette to include elements that are of particular importance in time-based media (motion graphics): sequence, pacing, framing and sound.
